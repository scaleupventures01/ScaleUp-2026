The Proof Test: Core Elements

Scope Limitation

A focused subset of the full engagement-typically 10-20% of scope-designed to validate the core hypothesis.

Success Criteria

Pre-defined, measurable criteria that must be met for the proof test to "pass" and trigger full engagement.

Risk Mitigation

If the proof test fails, the client has learned a valuable lesson at a fraction of full engagement cost.

Go/No-Go Decision

A formal decision point where both parties agree whether to proceed, pivot, or pause.

The Key Insight: The Proof Test isn't just phase 3-it's the philosophical core of The Certainty Method. Every phase exists to either prepare for, execute, or build upon the proof that our approach works for your specific situation.
