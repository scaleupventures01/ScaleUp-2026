Frequently Asked Questions

Q How quickly can we expect to see results?
It depends on the engagement. An assessment or strategic recommendation might be complete in days to weeks. A prototype or architecture might take a few weeks. I will set clear expectations during scoping and keep you informed throughout.
Q What if our priorities change during the engagement?
Business needs evolve. If priorities shift significantly, let me know and we can discuss adjusting scope. I prefer honest conversations about changing needs over continuing work that no longer serves you.
Q How do you handle confidential information?
I take confidentiality seriously. I sign NDAs as standard practice and maintain strict discretion about all client work. I am happy to accommodate any specific confidentiality requirements you have.
Q What happens if I am not satisfied with progress?
Tell me directly. I value honest feedback and would rather address concerns immediately than let issues compound. If something is not working, we will figure out how to get back on track together.
Q Do you work with our internal teams?
Yes, when it makes sense. I can collaborate with your developers, participate in relevant meetings, or work independently depending on what the engagement requires. We will establish the right approach during scoping.
Q What if you recommend we do not build something?
That is part of the value I provide. Sometimes the best outcome is clarity that a particular path is not worth pursuing. I have saved clients significant money by recommending against builds that would not serve their goals. You are paying for honest assessment, not just implementation.
Q What is included in our engagement, and what requires additional scope?
I define scope clearly upfront. If opportunities arise that fall outside the current engagement, I will discuss them with you before any additional work begins. No surprises.
