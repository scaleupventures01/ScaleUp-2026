Handling Materials During Transition

Transition Period Policy

During the rollout period, some materials will still contain the old tagline. Here's how to handle mixed-brand situations:

  • Digital materials: Update immediately - no grace period
  • Existing print stock: May be used until depleted (up to 90 days)
  • Client-facing materials: Prioritize updates for active deals
  • Internal materials: Can use old materials until next print run

What to Do When Asked About the Old Tagline

"I noticed your materials say 'We Prove It Before You Buy' - is that still your tagline?"

Recommended Response:

"We've recently evolved our brand positioning to 'Certainty First.' - a more focused expression of our core value. The materials you have reflect our previous messaging, but our commitment to eliminating risk before you invest remains exactly the same. In fact, it's now even more central to everything we do."

Disposal Guidelines for Old Materials

  • Digital Files Archive old versions in "Legacy" folder, remove from active circulation
  • Business Cards Use until depleted or recycle if brand consistency is critical
  • Printed Collateral Use for internal purposes, recycle for external use
  • Signage/Banners Replace before next public use
