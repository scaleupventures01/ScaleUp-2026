Worth thinking about. — Calvin P.S. If you're curious what your real constraint might be, reply with a one-sentence description of your biggest growth challenge. I read every response.

{{FirstName}}, Here's a question I ask every founder I work with: "What percentage of your revenue comes from expansion—upsells, cross-sells, and add-ons to existing customers?" The fix is usually straightforward: → Map what else your customers need that you could provide → Build systematic touchpoints to identify expansion timing → Create packaging that makes upgrades natural, not pushy In our ROI sessions, expansion revenue is one of the six factors we analyze.
