Implementation Checklist

CTA Hierarchy

  • Primary: High-value actions (Strategy Call, Custom Analysis)
  • Secondary: Mid-value actions (Download, Watch Video)
  • Tertiary: Low-commitment actions (Browse, Learn More)

CTA Copy Formulas

  • Outcome-focused: "Get Your [Outcome]"
  • Action-focused: "Calculate Your [Metric]"
  • Identity-focused: "Become Our Next [Success Type]"
  • Comparison-focused: "See How You Compare"

Placement Strategy

  • Hero: Result-led primary CTA
  • 25% scroll: ROI calculator or qualifier
  • 50% scroll: Social proof + CTA combo
  • End: Multi-option ladder CTA
  • Sticky: Floating bar with primary action

Testing Priorities

  • Outcome-focused vs. action-focused copy
  • Single CTA vs. multi-option ladder
  • Static vs. personalized messaging
  • Direct conversion vs. lead magnet first

Case study CTAs are the bridge between proof and action. Each design transforms evidence into opportunity, connecting documented success to prospect potential.
