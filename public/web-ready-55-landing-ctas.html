Implementation Guidelines

  • Primary CTAs: Gold background (#D4A853) with Navy text (#0F1729). Used for main conversion actions.
  • Secondary CTAs: Outline style or Navy background. Used for supporting actions.
  • Honest Language: No fake statistics, no invented client counts, no fabricated growth percentages.
  • Real Services: Build Assessment, Startup Support, Technical POC, AI Automation Agents, Full Product Launch (Financial Trading App, HR Automation Tool).
  • Button Sizing: Large (16px 40px padding) for hero/footer CTAs. Standard (14px 32px) for inline CTAs.
  • Tracking: All URLs include source/service parameters for attribution tracking.
  • Mobile: Stack buttons vertically on mobile. Primary CTA should always be visible without scrolling.
  • Hover States: Lighten gold to #e0bc6e, darken navy to #1a2744 on hover.
