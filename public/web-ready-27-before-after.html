Our Two-Track ROI Model

Every engagement follows one of two tracks, each with a distinct approach to proving value:

  • Track 1 - Ad Campaigns: We quantify projected ad spend AND projected leads BEFORE starting to ensure expected results meet client needs. If we miss targets, our fee is refunded (not ad spend)
  • Track 2 - App & Development Projects: Three-scenario ROI projections (high/mid/low) showing return in cash OR company/asset value

The case studies below demonstrate both tracks in action - from ad-driven lead generation to full product builds with measurable value creation.

1

No-Build Decision

Strategic Advisory Capital preservation through honest assessment
Before

Ready to invest $150K+ in unvalidated web app

After

Saved $150K+ and 12 months, capital redirected

The transformation: Client had a $150,000+ budget and 12-18 month commitment ready for a web app build. ScaleUp proposed a $5,000 discovery engagement (4 weeks) instead. Through rigorous validation, we found market saturation, hidden costs, and that 80% of the desired functionality was achievable with existing solutions. Result: Saved $150,000+ and 12+ months of runway. "'Certainty First' means we'd rather earn $5,000 telling the truth than $150,000 building something that won't serve you."
