Detailed Canvas Components

⚙

Customer Jobs

What 30-55 year old business owners are trying to accomplish

  • Generate consistent leads: Stop relying on word-of-mouth that has slowed down
  • Compete with digital-first rivals: Match the online presence of younger competitors
  • Modernize without disruption: Update technology without breaking what works
  • Increase efficiency: Do more with the same or fewer staff
  • Protect legacy: Ensure the business they built survives and thrives
  • Plan for exit: Maximize business value for eventual sale or succession
  • Stay relevant: Not be left behind by industry changes
  • Maintain relationships: Preserve the personal touch that built their success
⚠

Pains

Frustrations, obstacles, and risks they face

  • Burned by agencies: ~50% have been disappointed by marketing vendors before
  • Technology overwhelm: Cannot understand or evaluate tech solutions themselves
  • Cannot prove ROI: 81% struggle to measure marketing effectiveness
  • Fear of AI disruption: 90% worry AI will negatively impact their business
  • Hidden fees and scope creep: 40% of projects exceed budget
  • Vendor lock-in: Agencies hold assets hostage for ongoing payments
  • Outdated credibility: Website hurts perception with prospects
  • Losing to competition: Watching market share erode to digital-native rivals
★

Gains

Desired outcomes and benefits they seek

  • Measurable growth: Quantifiable increase in leads and revenue
  • Peace of mind: Confidence that technology is handled correctly
  • Competitive presence: Digital experience that matches or exceeds competitors
  • Time freedom: Less time managing technology, more on core business
  • Trust in partner: Reliable vendor they can depend on long-term
  • Exit readiness: Business positioned for maximum sale value
  • Industry leadership: Seen as modern, relevant, forward-thinking
  • Operational efficiency: AI and automation saving time and money
❖

Using the Canvas in Sales

The Value Proposition Canvas is a powerful sales tool when used correctly. Here's how to integrate it into the ScaleUp Ventures sales process to build trust and demonstrate understanding.

1

Discovery Call

Use the Customer Profile side to validate and uncover their specific pains and gains.

"Let me share what we typically hear from [industry] owners like you. You tell me if this resonates..."
2

Pain Validation

Walk through the pains list and note which ones resonate most strongly.

"Many owners tell us they've been seeking certainty before. Has that been your experience?"
3

Gain Prioritization

Identify which gains matter most to them personally - exit value? Time freedom? Competitive parity?

"If we could solve one thing for your business this year, what would matter most?"
4

Value Map Presentation

Show how ScaleUp's offerings specifically address their prioritized pains and gains.

"Based on what you've shared, here's exactly how we'd help you [specific pain relief]..."

Key Discovery Questions by Canvas Segment

Customer Jobs Questions

  • "What's the biggest challenge you're trying to solve this year?"
  • "How are you currently generating new business?"
  • "What does success look like for your business in 3 years?"
  • "Are you thinking about an eventual exit or transition?"

Pains Questions

  • "Have you worked with marketing agencies before? How did that go?"
  • "What's your biggest frustration with technology in your business?"
  • "How do you currently measure if your marketing is working?"
  • "What keeps you up at night about your business?"

Gains Questions

  • "If you woke up and everything was working perfectly, what would be different?"
  • "What would your business look like if you didn't have to worry about technology?"
  • "How much time per week do you spend on marketing-related tasks?"
  • "What would it mean for your business to be seen as a leader in your market?"

Transition to Value Map

  • "Based on what you've shared, here's what we typically do for [industry] owners..."
  • "The reason we focus on [pain] is because we've seen it directly impact [gain]..."
  • "Unlike typical agencies, we actually [differentiator] so you [outcome]..."
  • "Before we'd move forward, we'd build a custom ROI model so you see exactly how you make your money back."

The ScaleUp Advantage: Using the Canvas to Close

The canvas reveals our core differentiator naturally. After exploring pains (especially "seeking proof" and "can't prove ROI"), the transition to our pre-engagement ROI model becomes inevitable:

"You mentioned you've been seeking proof and you can't prove if marketing is working. That's exactly why we do things differently. Before you spend a dollar with us, we build a custom model showing exactly how you make your money back. You see the ROI before you commit. No other agency does this."
