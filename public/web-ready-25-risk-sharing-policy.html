Our Philosophy

Risk-Sharing Approach

We prove it before you buy - and if we can't, we'll tell you.

As an early-stage consultancy, we don't have decades of case studies to point to. What we offer instead is genuine skin in the game through our Two-Track ROI model.

1

Why We Share Risk

We're a young consultancy. We don't have a decade of client logos or hundreds of success stories. What we do have is conviction in our approach and a willingness to prove it.

Most consultants deliver advice, send an invoice, and move on whether you succeed or not. We think that model creates misaligned incentives. If we're confident enough to recommend a path, we should be willing to have something at stake.

We prove it before you buy - and if we can't, we'll tell you. Our Two-Track model ensures aligned incentives: guaranteed lead numbers for ad campaigns (fee refunded if missed), and three-scenario ROI projections for development projects showing your return in cash OR company/asset value.

2

What Risk-Sharing Looks Like

Risk-sharing isn't one-size-fits-all. We tailor the structure to each engagement based on scope, timeline, and what success looks like for your specific situation.

Track 1: Ad Campaigns - Guaranteed Lead Numbers

For marketing and lead generation engagements, we quantify projected ad spend AND projected leads BEFORE starting to ensure expected results meet client needs. We guarantee specific lead numbers upfront. If we miss the target, our fee is refunded (not the ad spend). You pay for results, not effort.

Track 2: App & Development Projects - Three-Scenario ROI

For application development and strategic projects, we provide three-scenario ROI projections (high/mid/low) showing your expected return in cash OR company/asset value. Full transparency on what success looks like before you commit.

Honest Recommendations

We prove it before you buy - and if we can't, we'll tell you. Sometimes the right advice is "don't build this" or "this isn't the right time." We've told clients not to proceed when the data pointed that direction, even when it meant less work for us.

Being Honest About Limitations

We're building our track record. Here's what we can and can't promise:

We can't guarantee outcomes we don't control. If your team needs to execute on recommendations and doesn't, or if external factors change the landscape, that's outside our scope.

We're not a large firm with infinite resources. We're selective about the engagements we take because we need to be confident we can deliver value.

Each engagement's terms are specific. The risk-sharing structure for your project will be defined in your Statement of Work. This page describes our philosophy, not a blanket policy.

Honesty isn't always comfortable. If we think your idea won't work, we'll tell you. That might not be what you want to hear, but it's what you're paying for.

Common Questions

How does your Two-Track model work in practice?

For ad campaigns (Track 1), we guarantee specific lead numbers upfront. If we miss the target, our fee is refunded - though ad spend remains yours. For app and development projects (Track 2), we provide three-scenario ROI projections (high/mid/low) showing your expected return in cash OR company/asset value. Every engagement is different, but the principle is consistent: we prove it before you buy - and if we can't, we'll tell you.

Are you just a new firm trying to win business?

Yes, honestly. We're early-stage and we need to earn trust. But that's exactly why risk-sharing makes sense for both of us. You get reduced risk working with a newer firm, and we get the opportunity to prove ourselves. If we deliver, we build reputation. If we don't, we've earned nothing.

What kinds of projects are a good fit?

We work best on projects with clear deliverables: discovery assessments, prototype development, proof of concepts, architecture design, and strategic analysis. We're less suited for open-ended advisory relationships or ongoing staff augmentation.

What if my project doesn't fit your Two-Track model?

Most projects fit into either Track 1 (ad campaigns with guaranteed leads) or Track 2 (app/development with three-scenario ROI projections). For projects that don't fit neatly, we're flexible on structure as long as there's clarity on deliverables and how we'll measure return on investment - whether that's in cash or company/asset value.

Why should I trust a new consultancy?

You shouldn't trust us based on claims. That's the point. We prove it before you buy - and if we can't, we'll tell you. Start with a small engagement. See how we work. Our Two-Track model means you get either guaranteed leads with fee refund if missed, or clear ROI projections showing high/mid/low scenarios before you commit.
