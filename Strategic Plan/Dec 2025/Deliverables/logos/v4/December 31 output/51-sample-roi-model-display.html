9Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our ROI modeling approach

How accurate are these projections?

Our models have historically delivered within +/- 15% of the expected scenario 73% of the time. We validate our methodology by tracking actual outcomes against projections for every engagement. The conservative scenario is designed to represent a floor that we achieve 85%+ of the time.

What data do you need to build my model?

We need 12-24 months of sales performance data: win rates, deal sizes, cycle times, and pipeline metrics. Ideally this comes from your CRM, but we can work with spreadsheets if needed. We also conduct a 60-minute discovery call to understand qualitative factors that affect the model.

Is there a cost for the ROI model?

No. We build your ROI model at no cost and with no obligation. This is how we demonstrate value before asking for commitment. The model is yours to keep regardless of whether you choose to engage with us.

What happens if you don't hit the projections?

Our engagement terms include performance guarantees tied to the conservative scenario. If we don't achieve at least the conservative case, we continue working at no additional cost until we do. We put our fees at risk because we're confident in our methodology.

How is this different from other consultants' proposals?

Most consultants present capabilities and methodologies. We present a financial model built on your data with specific, measurable outcomes and explicit assumptions. You know exactly what you're buying before you buy it. No other firm we're aware of does this.

Can I share this model with my board or investors?

Absolutely. The model is yours. Many clients use it for internal business cases, board presentations, and investor communications. We can provide an executive summary version designed specifically for stakeholder communication if helpful.
