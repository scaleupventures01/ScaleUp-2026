  • Initial Outreach: Attach PDF version to introduction emails. Subject line: "Why we're not a typical agency (1-page PDF)"
  • First Meeting Leave-Behind: Print on quality stock. Hand to prospect at end of discovery call with: "This summarizes what we discussed. Take your time - I'll follow up Thursday."
  • Proposal Companion: Include as first page of any proposal package to set expectations before diving into details.
  • Referral Tool: Give 3-5 copies to satisfied clients: "If you know anyone who's been seeking certainty, this explains why we're different."
  • Trade Shows/Networking: Premium handout for qualified prospects only - don't mass distribute.
  • Website Download: Gated content behind email capture: "Download: Why We're Not a Typical Agency"
