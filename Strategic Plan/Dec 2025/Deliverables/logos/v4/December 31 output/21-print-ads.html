Copy Specifications & Usage Notes

Production Guidelines

For Designer Reference

Brand Colors

  • Navy (Primary): #0F1729 | Pantone 289 C | CMYK: 100, 80, 30, 50
  • Gold (Accent): #D4A853 | Pantone 7555 C | CMYK: 0, 25, 70, 17
  • White: For text on navy backgrounds

Typography Recommendations

  • Headlines: Georgia, Garamond, or similar classic serif
  • Body Copy: Georgia, Minion Pro, or similar readable serif
  • Tagline: Uppercase, letterspaced, same serif family
  • Contact Info: Sans-serif acceptable for contrast (Helvetica, Arial)

Tone & Voice

  • Direct and confident, never aggressive
  • Empathetic to past disappointments
  • Professional and trustworthy
  • Speaks as a peer, not a vendor
  • Avoids marketing jargon and buzzwords

Placeholder Elements to Complete

  • [PHONE NUMBER] - Primary business phone
  • [WEBSITE URL] - Main website address
  • [EMAIL ADDRESS] - Contact email
  • Logo files in appropriate formats (vector for print)
  • QR code if desired for print-to-digital tracking

Word Counts by Format

  • Full Page: ~250 words body copy
  • Half Page: ~100 words body copy
  • Quarter Page: ~50 words body copy
  • Newspaper: ~200 words body copy
  • Vertical Ads: ~150 words body copy each
