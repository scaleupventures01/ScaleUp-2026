Why Owner Platforms Matter

Business owners don't live on LinkedIn. They scroll Facebook during lunch, Instagram in the evening, and TikTok when they can't sleep. Meet them where they are.

Proof Points for Owner Platforms:

  • Lead Guarantee ($25-$33 CPL): Owners care about ROI and risk. "Guaranteed leads or your money back" resonates.
  • AI Agents (20-25 hrs/month saved): Time is their scarcest resource. Automation stories hit home.
  • SEC/NFA Platform: Use sparingly - shows capability but less relatable to SMB owners.
  • No-Build Decision: Perfect for storytelling - owners seek proof by vendors before.

Platform Comparison

Platform Best For Content Style Posting Frequency
Facebook Longer stories, testimonials, community Conversational, personal, story-driven 1x daily
Instagram Feed Visual proof, before/after, quotes Polished, aspirational, branded 4-5x weekly
Instagram Stories Behind-the-scenes, polls, quick tips Raw, authentic, interactive 3-5x daily
Instagram Reels Quick tips, trending hooks, personality Fast-paced, hook-driven, entertaining 3-4x weekly
TikTok Myth-busting, hot takes, relatable moments Authentic, unpolished, trend-aware 1-2x daily
Key Difference from LinkedIn:
  • LinkedIn = Professional credibility, thought leadership, B2B networking
  • Owner Platforms = Human connection, relatable stories, "they get me" moments
  • Same proof points, different packaging - lead with emotion, back with facts

Facebook Templates (6 Formats)

Facebook

The Client Story

Tell a real client story in a conversational way. Facebook rewards longer, engaging content.

Let me tell you about [Client description - "a staffing agency owner" / "a contractor in Phoenix"]. They came to us [timeframe] ago with a problem: [Their situation in plain language - "They were spending $5K/month on marketing and had no idea if it was working"] Sound familiar? Here's what we did: [Step 1 - what you actually did] [Step 2 - the key insight or change] [Step 3 - the result] The result? [Specific outcome - "$25-$33 per lead instead of $150+" / "20 hours back every month"] The best part? [What made it special - "We guaranteed the leads or they didn't pay our fee"] If you're a business owner dealing with [their problem], drop a comment or DM me. Happy to share what worked. --- EXAMPLE (Lead Guarantee): Let me tell you about a pest control company owner in Florida. They came to us 4 months ago with a problem: They'd seeking certainty from three different marketing agencies. Thousands of dollars spent. Fancy reports. But the phone wasn't ringing with new customers. Sound familiar? Here's what we did: First, we told them EXACTLY how many leads they'd get before they spent a dollar. Not estimates. Guarantees. Then we built their campaign with one rule: if we don't hit the number, they don't pay our fee. The result? $25-$33 per lead. Phone ringing. New customers every week. The best part? They finally trust their marketing because there's actual accountability. If you're a business owner who's been seeking certainty before, drop a comment or DM me. Happy to share what worked.
Why This Works on Facebook:
  • Story format keeps people reading (algorithm loves time-on-post)
  • "Sound familiar?" creates personal connection
  • Specific numbers build credibility without being salesy
  • Soft CTA (comment/DM) feels helpful, not pushy

We built AI that handles what your team shouldn't have to. This is what "Certainty First" actually looks like.

Does that mean we make less money sometimes? Yes. Does it mean our clients trust us? Also yes. Agree or disagree? The Question Post

Drive engagement by asking something business owners actually think about.

Honest question for business owners: [Your question - "What's the one task you wish you could delete from your week?"] I'm asking because [why you're curious - "we're always looking for patterns in what drains owners' time"]. For most of our clients, it's been [common answer - "the follow-up work - chasing updates, writing reports, tracking who did what"]. That's why we started building [what you did about it - "AI agents that handle the repetitive delegation work"]. But I'm curious what YOUR answer is. Drop it in the comments. No sales pitch - just genuinely want to know. --- EXAMPLE: Honest question for business owners: Have you ever seeking certainty from a marketing agency? I'm asking because almost every client we talk to has a story. Money spent. Reports delivered. But the phone didn't ring. For most of our clients, the issue was the same: no accountability. Agencies got paid whether results happened or not. That's why we started guaranteeing leads. Hit the number or don't pay our fee. But I'm curious about YOUR story. Drop it in the comments. No sales pitch - just genuinely want to know what happened.

Behind the Scenes

Show the human side of your work. Builds trust through transparency.

Behind the scenes of what we actually do: [Photo or description of real work moment] [Context - what's happening in the photo/moment] This is [what this represents - "the part of consulting nobody posts about"]. [Honest reflection - what you were thinking, what was hard, what mattered] [Connection to why you do this work] It's not always glamorous. But moments like this are why we do what we do. --- EXAMPLE: Behind the scenes of what we actually do: [Photo of whiteboard with client strategy] This is from a discovery session with a staffing agency last week. We spent 3 hours mapping out their lead flow, costs, and what "success" would actually look like for their business. Most consultants would've just sent a proposal. But we don't start work until we can show the client their numbers - projected spend, projected leads, and what happens if we miss. It's not always glamorous. But moments like this - where things get clear - are why we do what we do.

Instagram Reels Scripts

Reels

The "Wait What?" Reel

Pattern interrupt that stops the scroll. Contrarian take delivered fast.

15-30 seconds

Script Structure:

0-3 sec (HOOK)

"I told a client NOT to build their software..."

3-15 sec (STORY)

"They came to us with $150K ready to spend. 18-month project. We did a $5K discovery phase instead. Turns out? 80% of what they needed already existed."

15-25 sec (LESSON)

"We saved them $150K and a year of their life. Yes, we made less money. But guess who sends us referrals now?"

25-30 sec (CTA)

"That's what 'Certainty First' means. Follow for more."

Filming Tips:
  • Talking head, eye contact, good lighting
  • Hook text on screen for first 3 seconds
  • Add captions (most watch without sound)
  • End with clear CTA (follow/DM/link in bio)
Reels

The "Red Flag" Reel

Call out bad practices in your industry. Positions you as the trustworthy alternative.

20-40 seconds

Script Structure:

0-3 sec (HOOK)

"Red flags your marketing agency won't tell you..." (text on screen)

3-10 sec (FLAG 1)

"They can't tell you exactly how many leads you'll get"

10-17 sec (FLAG 2)

"They get paid the same whether your campaign works or not"

17-25 sec (FLAG 3)

"They send reports full of metrics but can't explain your cost per lead"

25-35 sec (FLIP)

"We do it different. Guaranteed leads. Miss the target, don't pay our fee. That's it."

35-40 sec (CTA)

"Follow for more no-BS marketing advice."

TikTok Scripts (Short-Form Video)

TikTok Best Practices:

  • Hook in 1-2 seconds: If you don't grab them immediately, they scroll
  • Authentic > Polished: TikTok rewards real over produced
  • Trending sounds: Use them when relevant (but don't force it)
  • Story format: Beginning, middle, end - even in 30 seconds
  • Text on screen: Most watch without sound

Content Repurposing Guide

Create once, publish everywhere. Here's how one piece of content becomes 5+.

Framework

One Story, Five Platforms

START WITH: One client story (e.g., No-Build Decision, Lead Guarantee result) LINKEDIN (Long-form): Full story with business context, lessons learned, professional framing (Use templates from #61 Social Media Templates) FACEBOOK (Conversational): Same story, casual tone, "Let me tell you about..." format (Use Template 1: The Client Story) INSTAGRAM CAROUSEL: Story broken into 6-8 slides with key takeaways (Use Carousel template) INSTAGRAM REEL / TIKTOK: 30-second version with hook, story, lesson (Use "Wait What?" or Story Time scripts) INSTAGRAM STORIES: 3-5 story sequence teasing the full story "New post about a client who almost wasted $150K..." --- EXAMPLE FLOW: 1. Write LinkedIn post about No-Build Decision (10 min) 2. Adapt to Facebook conversational format (5 min) 3. Pull quotes for Instagram graphic (5 min) 4. Outline carousel from key points (10 min) 5. Film 30-sec TikTok version (15 min) 6. Post Stories teasing content (5 min) Total: ~50 min for 6 pieces of content
Repurposing Rules:
  • LinkedIn first: Write the full version, then simplify for other platforms
  • Tone shifts: LinkedIn = professional, FB = conversational, TikTok = raw/authentic
  • Same proof, different packaging: The story stays the same, the delivery changes
  • Link between platforms: "Full breakdown on LinkedIn" / "More on TikTok"
