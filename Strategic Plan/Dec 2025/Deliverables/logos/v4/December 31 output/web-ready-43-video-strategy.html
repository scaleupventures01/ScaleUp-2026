Interview Questions (25 Total)

Opening Questions - Build Rapport 5 Questions
1
"Tell me about yourself and your business in a sentence or two."
Gets them comfortable on camera; provides context for viewers
2
"What does your company do, and who do you primarily serve?"
Establishes credibility and relatability for similar prospects
3
"How long have you been in business, and what's your current stage of growth?"
Helps prospects identify if they're at similar stages
4
"What's the most exciting part of running your business right now?"
Gets positive energy flowing before discussing challenges
5
"What initially drew you to consider outside advisory support?"
Soft transition into discussing their journey with ScaleUp
Challenge Questions - Identify Pain Points 5 Questions
6
"What was the biggest challenge you were facing before working with ScaleUp Ventures?"
Creates "before" contrast; prospects relate to shared struggles
7
"How was that challenge affecting your business day-to-day?"
Deepens the pain point; shows real impact
8
"What had you tried before that didn't work?"
Shows ScaleUp as the solution that finally worked
9
"What was the cost of not solving this problem - financially or otherwise?"
Quantifies the stakes; creates urgency for viewers
10
"Was there a breaking point that made you decide you needed help?"
Captures emotional turning point; highly relatable moment
Solution Questions - Highlight Experience 5 Questions
11
"How did you first hear about ScaleUp Ventures, and what made you reach out?"
Shows discovery path; validates marketing channels
12
"What was your first impression when you started working with Calvin?"
Personalizes the experience; builds trust in the relationship
13
"Can you describe a specific strategy or insight that made a real difference?"
Provides concrete examples of value; not vague praise
14
"How would you describe the advisory relationship compared to other services you've used?"
Differentiates ScaleUp from competitors
15
"Was there an 'aha moment' where everything started clicking?"
Captures transformation moment; emotionally compelling
Results Questions - Quantify Success 5 Questions
16
"What specific results have you achieved since working with ScaleUp Ventures?"
Get numbers if possible - revenue, growth %, time saved
17
"How has your business changed in the last 6-12 months?"
Shows trajectory and ongoing value
18
"What's possible now that wasn't possible before?"
Forward-looking; shows expanded capabilities
19
"How has this impacted you personally - not just the business?"
Emotional payoff; work-life balance, confidence, clarity
20
"If you could put a dollar value on what you've gained, what would it be?"
Two-Track ROI: Ad campaigns = we quantify projected ad spend AND projected leads BEFORE starting to ensure expected results meet client needs; App/development = three-scenario projections (high/mid/low) showing return in cash OR company value. Key message: "We prove it before you buy - and if we can't, we'll tell you"

Production Guidelines

Camera Equipment
  • Primary Camera
    Sony A7IV or equivalent mirrorless (4K, 60fps)
  • Lens
    35mm or 50mm f/1.8 for shallow depth of field
  • Tripod
    Fluid head tripod for smooth adjustments
  • Backup Option
    iPhone 15 Pro in Cinematic Mode (remote interviews)
Lighting Setup
  • Key Light
    Softbox at 45-degree angle, slightly above eye level
  • Fill Light
    Reflector or soft LED opposite the key light
  • Hair/Rim Light
    Subtle backlight to separate subject from background
  • Color Temperature
    5600K (daylight balanced) for natural look
Audio Requirements
  • Lavalier Microphone
    Rode Wireless GO II or Sennheiser AVX
  • Backup Audio
    Shotgun mic on camera as secondary track
  • Audio Levels
    Peak at -12dB to -6dB, never clip
  • Environment
    Quiet room, no HVAC noise, no echo
Background Options
  • Office Setting (Preferred)
    Subject's actual workspace with depth
  • Neutral Studio
    Navy backdrop matches brand, clean look
  • Depth of Field
    Blur background (f/2.8 or wider) to focus on face
  • Avoid
    Blank walls, cluttered spaces, windows behind

Technical Specifications

Specification Requirement
Resolution 4K (3840 x 2160) minimum, record in LOG for flexibility
Frame Rate 24fps for cinematic feel, 60fps for slow-motion B-roll
Aspect Ratio 16:9 master, crop to 9:16 and 1:1 for social
Target Length 60-90 seconds final edit (capture 15-20 mins raw)
Audio Format 48kHz, 24-bit uncompressed WAV
Export Codec H.265/HEVC for quality, H.264 for compatibility

Editing Framework

Video Structure Timeline (60-90 Seconds)

0:00 - 0:05

Hook Statement

Open with the most powerful soundbite. Example hook types: "They saved me from a $150K mistake" or "Calvin helped me see what I was missing." Use real client quotes only.
0:05 - 0:10

Intro + Name Lower Third

Brief introduction: Name, title, company. Add branded lower third with gold accent. Establish credibility.
0:10 - 0:25

The Challenge

What problem were they facing? What was at stake? Create relatable tension that prospects identify with.
0:25 - 0:45

The Solution + Experience

How did ScaleUp help? What was the experience like? Include specific strategies or moments that made a difference.
0:45 - 1:10

The Results

Quantifiable outcomes and emotional impact. Include on-screen text overlays for key metrics. Show transformation.
1:10 - 1:25

Recommendation + CTA

Who should work with ScaleUp? End with their strongest endorsement. Add branded end card with website/contact.

I hope this finds you well. I've been reflecting on our work together over the past [X months], and I'm genuinely proud of what you've accomplished - [specific achievement: e.g., "avoiding a costly development path" or "getting your MVP launched" or "clarifying your go-to-market strategy"].

Would you be open to sharing your experience in a short video testimonial?

It would mean a lot to me personally, and honestly, your story could help other founders who are facing the same challenges you were when we started.

What's involved:

  • 20-30 minute conversation (I'll guide you through it)
  • Can be done via Zoom or in person
  • No prep required - just be yourself
  • You'll approve the final edit before we use it

What you get in return:

  • Professional video you can use for your own marketing
  • Feature on our website and social channels (exposure to our audience)
  • A $200 Amazon gift card as a thank-you
  • My eternal gratitude

I completely understand if you're not comfortable on camera or if timing doesn't work - no pressure at all. But if you're open to it, just reply "I'm in" and I'll send over a few time options.

Either way, thanks for being such a great partner to work with.

Calvin Williams
Founder, ScaleUp Ventures
