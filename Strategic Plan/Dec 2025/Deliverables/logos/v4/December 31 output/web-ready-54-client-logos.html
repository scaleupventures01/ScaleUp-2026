Usage Guidelines

Implementation Guidelines

Best practices for building trust signals

Honest Positioning

  • Be Honest: Don't pretend to have more clients than you do. Authenticity builds more trust than fake logos.
  • Lead with Impact: Focus on the results you've delivered, not the volume of clients.
  • Highlight Confidentiality: Turn the lack of public logos into a positive - you protect client information.
  • Feature Your Client Work: Full product launches like the HR Automation Tool demonstrate your end-to-end capabilities.
  • Be Specific: "$150K+ saved" is more credible than vague claims about "transforming businesses."

What to Show vs. Hide

Do

  • Share anonymized impact metrics
  • Highlight your full product launches
  • Explain why work is confidential
  • Offer private conversations for details
  • Be specific about types of work
  • Show your methodology/approach

Don't

  • Use fake company names or logos
  • Claim clients you don't have
  • Make up statistics
  • Oversell your experience
  • Be anything other than honest
  • Copy enterprise-style logo bars

Where to Use Each Version

  • Work Highlights (White): Main services page, about page. Use when you want to lead with specific impact.
  • Work Highlights (Navy): Homepage section, after hero. Creates premium feel while being honest.
  • Minimal Trust Stats: Above the fold, footer, anywhere space is limited. Quick credibility signal.
  • Approach Cards: Services page, "Why Us" section. Explains value without needing logos.
  • Confidential Notice: FAQ section, case studies page. Addresses the "where are your clients?" question directly.

As You Grow

  • Get Permission: As you complete client work, ask if you can reference them publicly.
  • Build Case Studies: Even anonymized case studies with permission are valuable.
  • Collect Testimonials: Quotes don't require logos to be credible.
  • Track Your Impact: Keep records of results so you can share specific numbers.
  • Update Regularly: As you gain more clients, update these sections accordingly.
