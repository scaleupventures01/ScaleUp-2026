Our Two-Track ROI Model: We Prove It Before You Buy

We prove it before you buy - and if we can't, we'll tell you.

Track 1 - Ad Campaigns: We quantify projected ad spend AND projected leads BEFORE starting to ensure expected results meet client needs. If we miss targets, our fee is refunded (not ad spend). You know exactly what you're getting before you commit.

Track 2 - App & Development Projects: Three-scenario ROI projections (high/mid/low) showing return in cash OR company/asset value. No black-box promises - you see the math and make informed decisions.

CR-001 Strategic Advisory

"Client saved $150K+ and 12+ months by choosing NOT to build"

What Happened

Client had $150,000+ budget and 12-18 month commitment planned. ScaleUp proposed $5,000 discovery (4 weeks). Our analysis found market saturation, hidden costs, and that 80% was achievable with existing solutions. Result: Saved $150,000+ and 12+ months.

Why This Matters

Sometimes the best advice is "don't do it." We prioritize client outcomes over our own revenue. Our philosophy: "'Certainty First' means we'd rather earn $5,000 telling the truth than $150,000 building something that won't serve you."
