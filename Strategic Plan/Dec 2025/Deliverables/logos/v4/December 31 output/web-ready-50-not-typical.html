Client had $150,000+ budget and 12-18 month commitment ready. ScaleUp proposed $5,000 discovery (4 weeks) instead. We found market saturation, hidden costs, and that 80% was achievable with existing solutions. Saved $150,000+ and 12+ months.

Philosophy: "'Certainty First' means we'd rather earn $5,000 telling the truth than $150,000 building something that won't serve you."
