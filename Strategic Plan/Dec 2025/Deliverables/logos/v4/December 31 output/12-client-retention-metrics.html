2. Metric Calculations

Precise formulas for calculating each retention metric with examples

Retention Rate Formula

Formula
Retention Rate = ((Clients at End - New Clients) / Clients at Start) x 100
Example: ((47 - 5) / 45) x 100 = 93.3%
Started with 45 clients, ended with 47, acquired 5 new = 42 retained out of 45

Calculation Guidelines:

  • Calculate on rolling 12-month basis for accuracy
  • Count only active engagements (exclude paused)
  • Segment by client tier for meaningful analysis
  • Track both logo retention and revenue retention separately

Expansion Rate Formula

Formula
Expansion Rate = (Clients Who Expanded / Total Retained Clients) x 100
Example: (34 / 42) x 100 = 80.9%
Of 42 retained clients, 34 expanded their engagement = 80% expansion rate

What Counts as Expansion:

Expansion Type Threshold Counts As
Revenue increase >10% YoY growth Yes
New service line Any addition Yes
Contract extension >12 months Yes
Referral provided Qualified lead Yes
Flat renewal Same scope/value No

Lifetime Value (LTV) Formula

Formula
LTV = Average Annual Revenue x Average Client Lifespan x Gross Margin
Example: $36,000 x 4.2 years x 0.85 = $128,520
Average retainer of $3K/month for 4.2 years at 85% margin

Component Calculation:

  • Average Annual Revenue: Total revenue / Active clients
  • Average Lifespan: Sum of tenure / Total clients
  • Gross Margin: (Revenue - Direct Costs) / Revenue

LTV:CAC Ratio Target:

3:1

Minimum healthy ratio for advisory services. ScaleUp Ventures maintains 5:1 LTV:CAC ratio.

Net Promoter Score (NPS) Calculation

Formula
NPS = % Promoters (9-10) - % Detractors (0-6)
Example: 80% Promoters - 8% Detractors = 72 NPS
Survey responses: 40 promoters, 6 passives, 4 detractors = 50 total

NPS Survey Best Practices:

  • Survey quarterly for trending data
  • Include follow-up question: "What's the primary reason for your score?"
  • Target 50%+ response rate for validity
  • Segment by client tenure and engagement type
  • Close the loop: Follow up with detractors within 48 hours

Design Guidelines

Color Usage

  • Use Gold (#D4A853) for key metrics and highlights
  • Navy (#0F1729) for backgrounds and text
  • White for contrast on dark backgrounds
  • Gray tones for supporting information

Typography

  • Bold, large numbers (48-72px for heroes)
  • Uppercase labels with letter-spacing
  • Clean sans-serif fonts (Segoe UI, system fonts)
  • High contrast for accessibility

Accessibility Note

Ensure all visual displays maintain a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for text. The Gold (#D4A853) on Navy (#0F1729) combination meets WCAG AA standards. Always include text alternatives when using icons or graphics to convey metrics.
