Questions Before We Talk?

Here's what business owners typically want to know.

What makes ScaleUp Ventures different?

I'm a single founder who works directly with every client. No account managers, no handoffs, no junior consultants doing the actual work. When you hire ScaleUp Ventures, you get me - and I'll be honest about what you actually need, even if that means telling you not to build something.

What kind of clients do you work with?

I work best with startups and growing companies that need strategic technology leadership without the cost of a full-time executive. Whether it's helping decide if a custom build makes sense, setting up PM infrastructure, or providing fractional CTO services, I focus on delivering real value for your specific situation.

What's your approach to engagements?

I believe in the "Certainty First" philosophy. Before recommending any path forward, I'll help you understand the full picture - including when the right answer is not to build something. I've saved clients over $150K by helping them avoid unnecessary custom development. The right answer isn't always the most exciting one.
