ScaleUp Ventures

Certainty First.
Executive Brand Summary December 2025 | Confidential
1
Brand Essence
Who We Are
A selective advisory practice working with a small number of service businesses ready to scale with certainty.
What We Do
We model, prove, and execute growth strategies before you invest a dollar.
Our Approach
Intentionally selective. We take on few engagements to ensure deep partnership with each client.
2
Target Audience
Primary:
Business owners, 30-55, naturally skeptical of consultants
Revenue:
$1M - $15M annually
Industries:
Staffing Veterinary Skilled Trades Insurance Pest Control
3
Positioning
"Certainty First."
"We prove it before you buy - and if we can't, we'll tell you."
Philosophy
Selective by design. Deep engagement over volume. ROI modeling before commitment.
Two-Track ROI Model
Track 1 - Ad Campaigns: We quantify projected ad spend AND projected leads BEFORE starting to ensure expected results meet client needs. Guaranteed lead numbers, fee refunded (not ad spend) if missed.
Track 2 - App & Development: Three-scenario ROI projections (high/mid/low) showing return in cash OR company/asset value
AI Agents: 20-25 Hours Saved Monthly
The Solution: Built AI automation agents for a consulting firm that handle delegated work.
The Impact: Time savings that compound - 20-25 hours in month one, growing as teams find more processes to automate.
The Result: Measurable efficiency gains that compound over time.
