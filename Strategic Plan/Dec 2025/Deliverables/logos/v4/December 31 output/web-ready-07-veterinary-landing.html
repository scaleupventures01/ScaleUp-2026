The Challenges You Face

Running a veterinary practice has never been harder

Between corporate consolidation, rising costs, and the constant struggle to find good staff, independent practice owners face pressures from every direction.

New Patient Acquisition

Pet owners have more choices than ever. Between online reviews, social media, and the corporate clinic that just opened down the street, attracting new clients requires more than word-of-mouth and a good reputation.

Corporate Chain Competition

VCA, Banfield, and private equity-backed groups have deeper pockets, bigger marketing budgets, and aggressive expansion plans. They are acquiring practices and squeezing independents on price, convenience, and visibility.

Staff Retention Crisis

Finding qualified veterinary technicians and associates is a constant battle. Burnout is real, turnover is expensive, and the shortage of vet school graduates means competition for talent is fierce.
