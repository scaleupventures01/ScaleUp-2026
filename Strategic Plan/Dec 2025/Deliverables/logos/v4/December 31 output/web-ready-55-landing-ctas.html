Implementation Guidelines

  • Primary CTAs: Gold background (#D4A853) with Navy text (#0F1729). Used for main conversion actions.
  • Secondary CTAs: Outline style or Navy background (#0F1729) with white text. Used for supporting actions.
  • Brand Philosophy: "Certainty First" - honest guidance for established business owners. No fake statistics or fabricated growth claims.
  • Real Services (3):
    • Web Application Development: /contact?service=web-development
    • Growth Marketing: /contact?service=growth-marketing
    • AI Agent Development: /contact?service=ai-agents
  • Button Sizing: Large (16px 40px padding) for hero/footer CTAs. Standard (14px 32px) for inline CTAs.
  • Tracking: All URLs include source/service parameters for attribution tracking.
  • Mobile: Stack buttons vertically on mobile. Primary CTA should always be visible without scrolling.
  • Hover States: Lighten gold to #e0bc6e, darken navy to #1a2744 on hover.
  • Response Time: Contact page includes trust element: "We typically respond within 4 business hours"
