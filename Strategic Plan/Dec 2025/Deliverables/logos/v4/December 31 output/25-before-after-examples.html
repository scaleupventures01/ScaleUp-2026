Quick Reference: ScaleUp Ventures Voice Principles

Watchful
We observe before we act. Copy shows pattern recognition, deep understanding, and the patience to see what others miss.
Measured
Every word is intentional. We avoid hyperbole, urgency tactics, and empty claims. Calm confidence over aggressive selling.
Decisive
When we speak, we speak with conviction. Clear positions, direct language, no hedging or vague generalities.
Certainty First
Our tagline embodies everything. We build confidence through evidence, patience, and expertise - never through pressure.
1

Off-Brand to On-Brand Headlines

Headlines set the tone. ScaleUp headlines are confident without being aggressive, specific without being limiting.

Homepage Hero Website
X Before
"We're the BEST Venture Capital Firm for YOUR Startup!"
✓ After
"Patient capital. Measured decisions. Exceptional outcomes."
Why This Works
The original uses aggressive superlatives and addresses the founder directly in a pushy way. The revision embodies the Measured voice - stating values as facts rather than making comparative claims. The rhythm of three creates confidence without desperation.
Investment Philosophy Website
X Before
"We Move Fast and Break Things to Find Unicorns!"
✓ After
"We watch. We wait. We move when the moment is right."
Why This Works
The Watchful attribute means we don't rush. The original copies Silicon Valley cliches that contradict our patient approach. The revision shows deliberate restraint culminating in decisive action - the full arc of our investment philosophy.
Value Proposition Website
X Before
"Disrupting the VC Industry with Revolutionary Approaches"
✓ After
"Certainty in an uncertain market."
Why This Works
"Disrupting" and "Revolutionary" are empty buzzwords that sound like every other VC. The revision is Decisive - it makes a clear, specific promise tied directly to our tagline. It's differentiated by being understated.
2

Generic to Specific Copy

Vague claims feel like marketing. Specific details feel like expertise.

Track Record About Page
X Before
"We have a proven track record of success with many successful investments."
✓ After
"23 investments. 7 exits. 4.2x average return. Every number tells a story of patience rewarded."
Why This Works
The Watchful voice pays attention to detail - and shares those details. "Proven track record" says nothing; specific numbers demonstrate the pattern recognition and analytical rigor that defines our approach.
Support Description Services Page
X Before
"We provide hands-on support and resources to help founders succeed."
✓ After
"Monthly strategy sessions. On-call CFO access. Board preparation. Hiring introductions within 48 hours."
Why This Works
Generic promises are forgettable; specific commitments are memorable. The revision shows exactly what "support" means in practice. This is Decisive communication - no ambiguity about what founders receive.
3

Passive to Active Voice

The Decisive attribute requires direct language. Passive voice hedges; active voice commits.

Investment Decision Process Page
X Before
"Investment decisions are made by our partnership committee after careful review."
✓ After
"Our partnership reviews every opportunity. We decide within 30 days. No founder waits in limbo."
Why This Works
Passive voice ("are made") distances us from accountability. Active voice ("We decide") takes ownership. The final sentence shows empathy and commitment - we understand what matters to founders.
Due Diligence Process Page
X Before
"Thorough due diligence is conducted on all potential portfolio companies."
✓ After
"We study every angle. Market dynamics. Competitive positioning. Unit economics. Team capability. Nothing escapes our review."
Why This Works
The Watchful voice shows what we actually observe. The active "We study" combined with specific areas of focus demonstrates the thoroughness rather than just claiming it. "Nothing escapes" is confidently Decisive.
4

Jargon-Heavy to Plain English

Clarity is confidence. Jargon often masks uncertain thinking.

Investment Thesis Thesis Page
X Before
"We leverage synergistic value-add capabilities to optimize portfolio company runway extension and accelerate path-to-profitability metrics."
✓ After
"We help companies spend less and earn more. It starts with knowing where to look."
Why This Works
The jargon version sounds like it's trying to impress. The plain version sounds like expertise. A Measured voice doesn't need buzzwords - clear thinking leads to clear language. The second sentence hints at our Watchful methodology.
Market Analysis Blog Post
X Before
"The current macroeconomic headwinds necessitate a recalibration of deployment velocity vis-a-vis valuation multiple compression."
✓ After
"Markets are uncertain. Valuations are down. This is when patience pays off most."
Why This Works
Complex ideas can be expressed simply without losing meaning. The revision is just as sophisticated in its insight but accessible to any reader. The Measured approach doesn't hide behind complexity.
5

Aggressive to Measured Tone

Urgency tactics undermine trust. Calm confidence attracts better partners.

Call to Action Website
X Before
"ACT NOW! Limited spots available! Don't miss your chance to work with the best!"
✓ After
"When you're ready, we're here. Let's start a conversation."
Why This Works
Urgency creates the wrong relationship from the start. The Measured voice respects the founder's timing. "When you're ready" shows we understand that building a company is a process - rushing benefits no one.
Competitive Positioning About Page
X Before
"Unlike other VCs who abandon founders after writing a check, WE actually deliver!"
✓ After
"Our involvement doesn't end at investment. It's where it begins."
Why This Works
Attacking competitors is desperate. The Measured voice focuses on what we do, not what others don't. The revision makes the same point with dignity - showing rather than shouting our difference.
Market Position Website
X Before
"We're CRUSHING the competition and dominating the early-stage market!"
✓ After
"Focused on early-stage. Built for long-term relationships."
Why This Works
Violence metaphors ("crushing," "dominating") conflict with our Watchful, patient brand. The revision is Decisive about our focus without aggression. It implies success through strategic clarity.
6

Vague Claims to Proof-Based Claims

"Certainty First" means every claim should have evidence behind it.

Portfolio Impact About Page
X Before
"We've helped countless founders achieve their dreams and build amazing companies."
✓ After
"42 founders. $380M in follow-on funding raised. 3 IPOs. Behind every number, a partnership built on trust."
Why This Works
"Countless" and "amazing" are meaningless. The Watchful voice observes patterns and shares data. Specific metrics create certainty for readers. The human element in the second sentence balances the numbers.
Expertise Claim Team Page
X Before
"Our team brings extensive experience and deep industry knowledge."
✓ After
"3 former founders. 2 Fortune 500 executives. 47 years combined investing experience. We've been where you're going."
Why This Works
Generic experience claims are unverifiable. The revision provides specific, checkable proof points. The final sentence connects credentials to founder relevance - Decisive empathy.
7

Feature-Focused to Benefit-Focused

Features describe us. Benefits describe what founders gain. Lead with outcomes.

Network Access Services Page
X Before
"We have an extensive network of 500+ industry contacts and advisors."
✓ After
"Need an intro to a Fortune 500 buyer? A technical co-founder? A specialized lawyer? One call. We'll connect you within 48 hours."
Why This Works
500+ contacts is a feature. Solving a founder's specific problem within 48 hours is a benefit. The revision shows scenarios founders actually face, demonstrating Watchful understanding of their needs.
Capital Structure Investment Page
X Before
"We have a $150M fund and can write checks from $500K to $5M."
✓ After
"Start with what you need. Grow with consistent follow-on. Our capital is patient - we're built for your timeline, not ours."
Why This Works
Fund size is our feature. Flexible, patient capital aligned with founder timelines is the benefit. The revision emphasizes Measured partnership over transactional numbers.
8

"I/They" to "We/You" Pronoun Fixes

Pronouns signal relationship. "We" creates partnership; "You" centers the founder.

Partnership Description About Page
X Before
"I believe that startups succeed when they get the right support from their investors."
✓ After
"You build the company. We provide the support. Together, we create certainty."
Why This Works
"I" centers the investor; "they" distances from founders. The revision uses "You" and "We" to create a direct, collaborative relationship. "Together" reinforces the partnership model.
Investment Philosophy Thesis Page
X Before
"ScaleUp Ventures looks for companies that have strong fundamentals and clear paths to profitability."
✓ After
"We look for strong fundamentals and clear paths to profitability. If that's how you build, we should talk."
Why This Works
Third-person references to our own company feel corporate and distant. "We" creates presence; "you" invites the reader in. The final sentence is Decisively direct about next steps.
9

Rushed to Patient Tone

The Watchful voice takes its time. Rushed copy signals anxiety; patient copy signals confidence.

Application Process Apply Page
X Before
"Apply today! Hurry - we're reviewing applications NOW and filling our portfolio FAST!"
✓ After
"Great companies are built on great timing. When you're ready to explore partnership, we'll take the time to understand your vision fully."
Why This Works
Urgency language ("Hurry," "NOW," "FAST") contradicts our Measured brand. The revision honors timing and signals thorough evaluation. "Take the time" explicitly values patience.
Decision Timeline Process Page
X Before
"We can turn around term sheets in just 72 hours! Lightning fast decisions!"
✓ After
"We take 30 days to decide - enough time to be thorough, not so long you're left waiting. Good decisions shouldn't be rushed."
Why This Works
Speed can signal carelessness. The revision frames our timeline as a feature - showing Watchful diligence while respecting founder time. The final sentence states our philosophy Decisively.
10

Emotional Temperature Adjustments

ScaleUp's emotional register is warm but controlled - confident without being cold, engaged without being effusive.

Welcome Message Email
X Before
"OMG we are SO EXCITED to meet you!!! This is going to be AMAZING!!! We literally can't wait!!!"
✓ After
"We're looking forward to our conversation. Your approach to [specific element] caught our attention - we'd like to understand it better."
Why This Works
Excessive enthusiasm feels performative and unprofessional. The Measured voice shows genuine interest through specificity, not exclamation points. Notice the temperature is warm ("looking forward") but controlled.
Pass Communication Email
X Before
"Unfortunately we have decided not to proceed with your company at this time. Best of luck."
✓ After
"After careful consideration, we've decided this isn't the right fit for our current portfolio focus. Specifically, [reason]. We respect what you're building and remain open to reconnecting as your company evolves."
Why This Works
Cold rejections damage relationships. The Watchful revision shows we actually evaluated thoughtfully. Providing a specific reason demonstrates respect. The door stays open - Measured, not final.

Website Copy Examples

Public-facing content requires the highest brand fidelity

About Section Opening Website
X Before
"ScaleUp Ventures is a leading early-stage venture capital firm dedicated to backing the next generation of world-changing companies and visionary entrepreneurs."
✓ After
"We invest early. We stay patient. We help companies become what they're meant to be. Since 2018, we've partnered with 42 founders who share our belief that sustainable growth beats fast exits."
Why This Works
Every phrase in the original could describe any VC. The revision opens with three Decisive statements that embody our voice, then provides specific proof. "What they're meant to be" subtly signals our Watchful, observational approach.
Footer CTA Website
X Before
"Ready to take your startup to the next level? Get in touch with our team today!"
✓ After
"Building something worth waiting for? So are we."
Why This Works
"Next level" is cliche; "today" adds unnecessary urgency. The revision filters for founders who share our patience. It's Measured (no pressure), Watchful (we recognize quality), and Decisive (clear value alignment stated).

Email Copy Examples

Direct communication where voice builds or breaks relationships

Initial Outreach Email
X Before
"Hi there! I came across your company and was super impressed! Would love to grab coffee and chat about how ScaleUp Ventures could potentially maybe be a fit for your funding needs!"
✓ After
"I've been following [Company] since your [specific milestone]. Your approach to [specific element] aligns with patterns we've seen in successful portfolio companies. Would you be open to a 30-minute conversation about your growth plans?"
Why This Works
The Watchful voice shows we've done our homework. Specific observations prove genuine interest. No hedging ("potentially maybe") - Decisive directness about the ask. Professional warmth replaces performative enthusiasm.
Follow-up After Meeting Email
X Before
"Great meeting you! You guys are doing awesome stuff! Let's definitely keep in touch and see if there might be something there!"
✓ After
"Thank you for walking us through your unit economics. Your path to profitability is clearer than most companies at your stage. We'll discuss internally and share next steps by Friday."
Why This Works
Generic praise is forgettable. The Watchful revision references specific discussion points, demonstrating attention. The Decisive commitment to a timeline ("by Friday") shows professionalism and respect for their time.
Portfolio Update Request Email
X Before
"Hey! It's been a while! Just checking in to see how things are going! Would love an update when you get a chance! No rush!"
✓ After
"Checking in on Q3 progress. Specifically interested in: (1) How the enterprise sales motion is developing, (2) Any changes to your hiring timeline. A brief update by month-end would be helpful for our portfolio review."
Why This Works
"No rush" contradicts the request for an update. The revision is Decisively specific about what we need and why, respecting the founder's time. The Watchful voice references their actual business context.

Proposal Copy Examples

High-stakes documents where precision and confidence close deals

Term Sheet Introduction Proposal
X Before
"We are thrilled and honored to present this incredible opportunity to potentially partner with your amazing team on this exciting journey!"
✓ After
"We've completed our evaluation. The following terms reflect our conviction in your company and our commitment to supporting your growth."
Why This Works
Excessive adjectives ("thrilled," "incredible," "amazing," "exciting") dilute the message. The Measured revision is businesslike and confident. "Conviction" and "commitment" are the only two charged words - chosen deliberately.
Investment Rationale Proposal
X Before
"We think you guys are onto something big here and believe this could be a massive opportunity in a huge market with tons of potential upside."
✓ After
"Three factors drive our investment thesis: (1) Your customer retention exceeds category benchmarks by 40%, (2) Your unit economics are already profitable, (3) The competitive moat you've built through [specific factor] is defensible."
Why This Works
Vague excitement ("big," "massive," "huge," "tons") says nothing. The Watchful revision shows we've analyzed specifically why we believe. Numbered points demonstrate structured thinking. Every claim is specific and provable.
Value-Add Section Proposal
X Before
"As your partner, ScaleUp Ventures will leverage our extensive platform capabilities and robust ecosystem to unlock synergistic value creation opportunities and accelerate your growth trajectory."
✓ After
"What we bring: Direct introductions to 12 enterprise customers in your target market. CFO office hours every month. Recruiting support - we've helped portfolio companies fill 200+ roles. Specifics matter more than promises."
Why This Works
Jargon ("synergistic value creation opportunities") is empty. The revision lists exactly what support looks like, with numbers. The final sentence is Decisively meta - acknowledging that our specificity is itself a proof point.
Closing Statement Proposal
X Before
"We really hope you'll give us the opportunity to partner with you on this incredible journey! Let us know ASAP as we're super excited to move forward!"
✓ After
"This term sheet is valid for 14 days. We're prepared to close within 30 days of signature. If you have questions, we're available to discuss. The decision is yours - we'll respect whatever you choose."
Why This Works
Desperation ("really hope," "ASAP," "super excited") undermines negotiating position. The Measured revision is clear about timelines without pressure. The final sentence shows confidence - we want the deal but don't need it.
