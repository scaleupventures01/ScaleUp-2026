What Is The Proof Test?

A two-track methodology that proves your value before you commit.

Definition

The Proof Test is a rigorous pre-engagement process with two tracks based on what you want to validate. Choose one or both:

Track 1 - Ad Campaigns: Guaranteed lead numbers with our fee refunded (not ad spend) if we miss targets. You know the outcome before you commit.

Track 2 - App & Development Projects: Three-scenario ROI projections (high/mid/low) showing return in cash OR company/asset value, stress-tested against real-world conditions.

The result: We prove it before you buy -- and if we can't, we'll tell you.

Why We Created It

  • Too many businesses invest millions based on projections that are little more than educated guesses
  • Traditional consulting asks you to commit before proving value -- we prove value before you commit
  • Some initiatives shouldn't proceed -- The Proof Test identifies these before you waste resources
  • When you do proceed, you'll have a validated roadmap with clear milestones and success metrics

Two-Track Modeling

Using our Two-Track framework, we build the value projections that form the core of The Proof Test. For ad campaigns: guaranteed lead numbers. For development projects: three-scenario ROI projections.

Track 1: Ad Campaigns

  • Quantify projected ad spend AND projected leads BEFORE starting
  • Ensure expected results meet client needs before commitment
  • Fee refund terms if targets are missed (ad spend excluded)
  • Campaign structure and timeline recommendations
  • Risk factors and mitigation strategies

Track 2: App & Development

  • Three-scenario projections (high/mid/low)
  • Return shown in cash OR company/asset value
  • Sensitivity analysis on key variables
  • Complete assumption documentation
4

Scenario Planning

We synthesize all findings into three distinct scenarios: Conservative, Moderate, and Optimistic. Each scenario is probability-weighted and includes specific conditions required to achieve those outcomes.

Best / Worst / Expected

  • Conservative: 70% confidence -- assumes challenges, slower adoption
  • Moderate: 50% confidence -- realistic execution with typical hurdles
  • Optimistic: 30% confidence -- best-case with favorable conditions
  • Each includes timeline, investment, and risk factors

What You Receive

  • Three-scenario comparison matrix
  • Risk assessment and mitigation strategies
  • Critical success factors documentation
  • Go/no-go recommendation with rationale
5

Decision Framework

A comprehensive presentation to your leadership team walking through every aspect of The Proof Test findings. You leave with complete clarity on whether to proceed -- and exactly what to expect if you do.

How Client Decides

  • Executive briefing with your leadership team
  • Interactive walkthrough of all three scenarios
  • Q&A session with our analysis team
  • Clear decision criteria and next steps
  • Implementation blueprint (if proceeding)

What You Receive

  • Executive presentation deck
  • Complete Proof Test documentation package
  • Board-ready summary report
  • Implementation roadmap (if go decision)

Frequently Asked Questions

What clients ask before starting The Proof Test.

What does The Proof Test cost?

+
The Proof Test is a fixed-fee engagement priced based on the complexity of your situation. For most early-stage and growth companies we work with, this typically ranges from $5,000 to $15,000. The fee is independent of any subsequent engagement -- if The Proof Test reveals that proceeding isn't advisable, you've avoided a much larger mistake. If you proceed with a full engagement, The Proof Test fee is credited toward your project investment.

What if The Proof Test shows we shouldn't proceed?

+
That's exactly the point -- and one of our most valuable outcomes. In one engagement, a $5K Proof Test saved a client over $150K by recommending they not build. We provide detailed documentation of why the initiative isn't advisable at this time, and what would need to change for it to become viable. Knowing when not to proceed is just as valuable as knowing when to move forward.

What data do you need from us?

+
We provide a detailed data request list tailored to your specific initiative. Typically this includes: historical operational metrics (12-24 months), financial data related to the areas of focus, current process documentation, and previous relevant analyses. We work with whatever format your data exists in and help identify gaps early in the process.

How accurate are your ROI projections?

+
We use a three-scenario approach specifically because single-point projections are often misleading. By providing conservative, moderate, and optimistic scenarios with clear assumptions for each, you can make decisions based on ranges rather than false precision. We're conservative in our estimates because we'd rather under-promise and over-deliver than the reverse.

Can we do The Proof Test before selecting a vendor?

+
Absolutely -- this is actually an ideal use case. The Proof Test can help you develop requirements, understand what ROI you should expect, and create evaluation criteria before you engage with vendors. This gives you significant leverage in negotiations because you'll know exactly what outcomes you should demand.

What happens after The Proof Test?

+
If our recommendation is to proceed, you'll have a complete implementation blueprint ready to execute. You can choose to engage ScaleUp Ventures for implementation support, use another firm, or handle it internally. The Proof Test deliverables are yours regardless. Many clients choose to continue with us for implementation -- we've helped startups with market validation, business/financial model validation, app development, fundraising plans, and investor/advisor intros.

How is our sensitive data protected?

+
All Proof Test engagements are covered by comprehensive NDAs. We use secure, encrypted file sharing systems, and all client data is destroyed 90 days after project completion unless you request otherwise. Our team members are bound by confidentiality agreements, and we never use client data for any purpose other than your specific engagement.

How much of our team's time is required?

+
We've designed The Proof Test to be minimally disruptive. Key stakeholders typically invest 4-6 hours total over the process. This includes an initial discovery session (2 hours), data access coordination (1-2 hours), and the final decision briefing (2 hours). We do the heavy lifting; you provide the access and context.
