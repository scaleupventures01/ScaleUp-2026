Strategic Advisory

The No-Build Decision

$150K+ Saved in Development Costs
Client Investment Protected

A client approached us with a $150,000+ budget and 12-18 month commitment for a custom web application. Instead of taking the project, we proposed a $5,000 discovery and feasibility analysis (4 weeks). Our analysis revealed: the market was more saturated than realized, hidden maintenance costs had not been budgeted, and existing solutions could accomplish 80% of their goals at a fraction of the cost.

Outcome: Client saved $150,000+ and 12+ months by NOT building. "Certainty First" is not a marketing slogan. It means we would rather earn $5,000 telling you the truth than $150,000 building something that will not serve you.
