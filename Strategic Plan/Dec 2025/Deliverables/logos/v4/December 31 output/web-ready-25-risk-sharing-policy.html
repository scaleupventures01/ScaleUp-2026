What Risk-Sharing Looks Like

Risk-sharing isn't one-size-fits-all. We tailor the structure to each engagement based on scope, timeline, and what success looks like for your specific situation.

★ Track 1: Guaranteed Leads or Your Money Back

Our flagship guarantee: For ad campaigns, we quantify projected ad spend AND projected leads BEFORE starting. We guarantee specific lead numbers upfront. If we miss the target, our fee is refunded (not the ad spend). You pay for results, not effort. We've proven this works with campaigns that validated market demand, customer fit, and profitable acquisition.

Track 2: App & Development Projects - Three-Scenario ROI

For application development and strategic projects, we provide three-scenario ROI projections (high/mid/low) showing your expected return in cash OR company/asset value. Full transparency on what success looks like before you commit - proven through builds like our SEC/NFA-compliant financial trading platform.

Honest Recommendations

We prove it before you buy - and if we can't, we'll tell you. Sometimes the right advice is "don't build this" or "this isn't the right time." We've told clients not to proceed when the data pointed that direction, even when it meant less work for us.

Integrity Note: When We Say "Don't Build"

Sometimes the right answer is to not proceed. In one case, a client had a $150,000+ budget ready. Our $5,000 discovery engagement revealed market saturation and that 80% of their needs were achievable with existing solutions. We saved them $150,000+ and 12+ months. "Certainty First" means we'd rather earn $5,000 telling the truth than $150,000 building something that won't serve you.

Being Honest About Limitations

Risk-sharing doesn't mean we control everything. Here's what we can and can't promise:

We can't guarantee outcomes we don't control. If your team needs to execute on recommendations and doesn't, or if external factors change the landscape, that's outside our scope.

We're selective about engagements. We only take work where we're confident we can deliver value. That selectivity is part of how we maintain our guarantee.

Each engagement's terms are specific. The risk-sharing structure for your project will be defined in your Statement of Work. This page describes our philosophy, not a blanket policy.

Honesty isn't always comfortable. If we think your idea won't work, we'll tell you. That might not be what you want to hear, but it's what you're paying for.

Common Questions

What does "Guaranteed leads or your money back" actually mean?

For ad campaigns (Track 1), we guarantee specific lead numbers upfront. If we miss the target, our fee is refunded - though ad spend remains yours. We've proven this works with guaranteed lead campaigns. For app and development projects (Track 2), we provide three-scenario ROI projections (high/mid/low) showing your expected return in cash OR company/asset value. Every engagement is different, but the principle is consistent: we prove it before you buy - and if we can't, we'll tell you.

Why do you share risk when most consultancies don't?

Because we've been on the client side. We've paid consultants for results and watched them move on regardless of outcome. That's frustrating. We built ScaleUp to align our interests with our clients' interests. When you succeed, we succeed. When we fail to deliver, we don't get paid. It's the right way to do business.

What kinds of projects are a good fit?

We work best on projects with clear deliverables: discovery assessments, prototype development, proof of concepts, architecture design, and strategic analysis. We're less suited for open-ended advisory relationships or ongoing staff augmentation.

What if my project doesn't fit your Two-Track model?

Most projects fit into either Track 1 (ad campaigns with guaranteed leads) or Track 2 (app/development with three-scenario ROI projections). For projects that don't fit neatly, we're flexible on structure as long as there's clarity on deliverables and how we'll measure return on investment - whether that's in cash or company/asset value.

How do I know you can deliver?

You shouldn't trust us based on claims. That's the point. We prove it before you buy - and if we can't, we'll tell you. Start with a small engagement. See how we work.
