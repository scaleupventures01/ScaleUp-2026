The ScaleUp Method

A four-phase methodology that transforms early-stage decisions from hopeful guesswork into evidence-based certainty.

Certainty First.

Why We Built This

Early-stage ventures face a critical challenge: making significant technology and growth investments based on incomplete information and optimistic assumptions.

They deserve better. They deserve certainty before commitment.

The ScaleUp Method emerged from our experience helping founders navigate build-vs-buy decisions, validate business models, and architect solutions for regulated industries. Every phase is designed to build confidence through evidence, not enthusiasm.

What Makes It Different

Most consultants sell solutions. We sell clarity—even when that means recommending you don't build. We prove it before you buy—and if we can't, we'll tell you.

  • Two-Track ROI: Guaranteed leads for ad campaigns (fee refunded if missed); three-scenario projections for development (high/mid/low)
  • Evidence before action: we've saved clients six figures by recommending no-build decisions
  • Returns measured in cash OR company/asset value
  • Transparent methodology you can verify yourself
  • Hands-on support from architecture to investor introductions
  • Experience across regulated industries, AI-enabled products, and startup operations

The Four Phases

From first conversation to measurable results, here's exactly how we work together.

1

Observe

The heron watches before it strikes

Before recommending any action, we invest time understanding your business with patience and precision. Like the heron, we don't chase—we observe, calculate, then act with certainty. This phase has led to no-build recommendations that saved clients significant investment.

What We Analyze

  • Revenue streams and profit margins
  • Customer acquisition and retention
  • Operational efficiency metrics
  • Competitive landscape
  • Market timing and conditions

Deliverables

  • Business health assessment
  • Opportunity identification report
  • Risk and constraint analysis
  • Prioritized focus areas
  • Go/no-go recommendation
Typical Duration: 2-3 Weeks
2

Model

Build the proof before the project

This is where certainty is built. We prove it before you buy—and if we can't, we'll tell you. Our Two-Track approach ensures you know exactly what to expect: guaranteed lead numbers for ad campaigns, or three-scenario projections for development work. Whether validating a business model for investor conversations or building a POC for a regulated industry, you'll see what success requires before major investment.

Two-Track ROI Model

  • Track 1 - Ad Campaigns: We quantify projected ad spend AND projected leads BEFORE starting to ensure expected results meet client needs. Fee refunded (not ad spend) if guaranteed lead numbers missed
  • Track 2 - App & Development: Three-scenario ROI projections (high/mid/low)
  • Returns measured in cash OR company/asset value
  • Break-even timeline calculations
  • Sensitivity analysis on key assumptions

Deliverables

  • Interactive financial model
  • Scenario comparison dashboard
  • Investment recommendation
  • Implementation roadmap
  • Success metrics definition
Typical Duration: 2-4 Weeks
3

Execute

The precise strike

With observation complete and models validated, we move into implementation. This can range from technical architecture and prototype development to building AI automation agents that handle delegated work—team members save 20-25 hours per month, with savings growing 10-20% monthly as more processes flow into the automated workflow. Focused execution on your highest-impact initiatives.

Implementation Approach

  • Weekly progress tracking

  • Stakeholder communication cadence

Reporting Cadence

  • Bi-weekly performance dashboards

  • Ad-hoc alerts on key metrics

Optimize

Continuous improvement, compounding returns

The best strategies evolve. For ongoing engagements, we continuously analyze performance, identify optimization opportunities, and help scale what works—including support for investor introductions and growth conversations.

Scaling Framework
