3

Words We Use / Words We Avoid

USE Proof and Trust Language

  • Model - "We build a model showing your ROI"
  • Documented - "Documented results from real clients"
  • Based on your numbers - Personal, concrete
  • Measurable - "Measurable outcomes, not promises"
  • Talk to our clients - Confidence signal
  • Honest assessment - Even if it's not what you want to hear
  • Accountable - "We're accountable to these numbers"
  • The math shows - Evidence over opinion
  • Payback period - Concrete timeline
  • We recommend against - Willing to say no

AVOID Hype and Consultant-Speak

  • Leverage - Corporate jargon, meaningless
  • Synergy - Red flag for skeptical owners
  • Holistic - Vague, overused
  • Revolutionary - Instant credibility killer
  • Game-changing - Empty superlative
  • Transform your business - Overpromising
  • Solutions - Generic, means nothing
  • Cutting-edge - Tech hype signal
  • Trust us - The words burned owners hate most
  • Act now - Pressure tactics backfire

Heron Narrative Bridge Statement

Primary Bridge Statement (For About Page)

Like the heron that stands patient in the shallows - watching, waiting, understanding every ripple before it strikes - ScaleUp Ventures does the work before you spend a dollar. We study your business, model your ROI, and prove our value before you commit. Because precision follows patience, and results follow proof.

Short Version (For Various Uses)

The heron waits, watches, then strikes with precision - we prove your ROI before you invest a dollar.

Extended Version (For Brand Story)

There is a reason we chose the heron as our symbol.

Watch a heron hunt and you will witness something remarkable: absolute stillness followed by flawless action. The heron does not chase. It does not guess. It stands motionless in the shallows - sometimes for hours - reading the water, tracking the light, understanding every pattern and possibility. Only when conditions align does it strike. And when it does, it rarely misses.

This is not passivity. This is preparation. This is the difference between hoping something works and knowing it will.

ScaleUp Ventures operates the same way. We do not rush to sell you a solution. We observe your business. We analyze your market. We model your ROI using real data. Before you invest a dollar, you see exactly how you make your money back.

Most agencies strike first and hope. We prove first and deliver.

That is the heron's way. That is our promise.
