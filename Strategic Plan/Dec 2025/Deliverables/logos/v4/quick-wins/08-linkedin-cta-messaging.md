# ScaleUp Ventures LinkedIn CTA Messaging

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Created:** December 31, 2025
**Purpose:** LinkedIn profile and company page messaging for founders and ScaleUp Ventures

---

## Brand Context

- **Tagline:** Proof First.
- **Core Differentiator:** Pre-engagement ROI modeling
- **Target Audience:** Business owners aged 50-65, $1M-$15M revenue, skeptical of agencies
- **Key Message:** "We show you how you'll make your money back before you spend a dollar."

---

## 1. Headline Options (120 characters max)

LinkedIn headlines appear directly under your name and are critical for first impressions. Each option below is calibrated to resonate with skeptical business owners who have been burned by agencies.

### Option A: ROI-First Positioning
```
We prove ROI before you spend a dollar | Helping $1M-$15M businesses modernize without the gamble
```
*Character count: 98*

**Why it works:** Leads with the core differentiator. "Before you spend a dollar" is the signature phrase that resonates with burned business owners. "Without the gamble" addresses their fear.

---

### Option B: Partner vs. Vendor Framing
```
Growth Partner for Technology Laggard Businesses | Dev + Marketing + AI Under One Roof
```
*Character count: 88*

**Why it works:** "Technology laggard" is an honest, non-condescending acknowledgment of the target market. "Under One Roof" addresses vendor fatigue.

---

### Option C: Outcome-Focused
```
I help business owners see the numbers before they commit | Co-Founder, ScaleUp Ventures
```
*Character count: 93*

**Why it works:** First-person, direct, and action-oriented. Centers the value proposition on transparency and decision-making confidence.

## 3. Featured Section Descriptions

The Featured section allows you to highlight key content. Each item should drive toward the core CTA: "See your ROI model before you commit."

---

### Case Study Feature

**Title:** How We Helped a Staffing Agency Double Lead Volume in 90 Days

**Description:**
> Robert ran his staffing firm for 25 years on referrals. But he was losing placements to competitors with modern websites and online applications. We showed him the ROI model first. Then delivered. Here's what happened.

**Why it works:** Specific persona (Robert) matches target audience. Concrete outcome (double lead volume) provides proof. Time-bound result (90 days) adds urgency.

---

### ROI Calculator / Assessment Feature

**Title:** Free ROI Assessment: See Your Numbers Before You Commit

**Description:**
> Before you invest in development, marketing, or automation, know what you'll get back. We'll build a model based on your actual business. No obligation. No jargon. Just math.

**Why it works:** Directly offers the core differentiator as a lead magnet. "No obligation" and "no jargon" address objections proactively.

---

### Thought Leadership Article Feature

**Title:** Why 81% of Business Owners Can't Prove Their Marketing ROI

**Description:**
> Most agencies sell deliverables, not outcomes. That's why so many owners feel burned. Here's what to ask before your next investment - and how to know if the numbers actually work.

**Why it works:** Uses validated statistic (81%) as hook. Positions ScaleUp as educator, not just vendor. Addresses the root cause of skepticism.

---

### Services Overview Feature

**Title:** Development + Marketing + AI: One Partner, One Plan

**Description:**
> Tired of managing five vendors who don't talk to each other? We integrate websites, growth marketing, and automation under one roof - with accountability for results.

**Why it works:** Addresses vendor fatigue. "Accountability for results" reinforces the brand promise.

## Usage Guidelines

### Voice Consistency Checklist

Before publishing any LinkedIn content, verify:

- [ ] Does it lead with outcomes, not capabilities?
- [ ] Does it include specific numbers or proof points?
- [ ] Would a 60-year-old business owner understand every word?
- [ ] Does it acknowledge their skepticism honestly?
- [ ] Is the CTA clear and low-pressure?

### Words to USE in LinkedIn Content

| Word | Why |
|------|-----|
| Proof | Core brand concept |
| ROI / Return | Differentiator language |
| Before you spend / Before you commit | Signature phrasing |
| Modernize | Non-threatening upgrade term |
| Results | Concrete, outcome-focused |
| Accountable | Sets us apart |

### Words to AVOID in LinkedIn Content

| Word | Why |
|------|-----|
| Solutions | Vague, overused |
| Cutting-edge | Intimidating to tech laggards |
| Synergy | Corporate jargon |
| Transform / Transformation | Abstract, overused |
| Disrupt | Negative association for target |
| Best-in-class | Unprovable superlative |
