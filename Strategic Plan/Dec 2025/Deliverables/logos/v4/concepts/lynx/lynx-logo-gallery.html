The Lynx - Keeper of Secrets

The lynx was revered as "the keeper of secrets" - seeing hidden truths others miss. Associated with Lynceus from Greek mythology who could see through the earth itself. The distinctive tufted ears, intense piercing eyes, and angular face represent the ability to see through darkness and deception - detecting what others miss.

Minimal - Piercing Eyes Only

Two intense, stylized lynx eyes that capture the essence of "seeing hidden truths." Gold irises represent valuable insight revealed. The most minimal interpretation, focused entirely on the penetrating gaze.

Profile View with Tufted Ears

Lynx head in left-facing profile with prominent tufted ears - the distinctive feature that separates lynx from other cats. Geometric and refined, like how Saul Bass or Paul Rand would render an animal.
