## Logo 1: Minimal Geometric Heron (Most Abstract)

```
Create a professional minimalist logo for "ScaleUp Ventures" featuring an abstract heron silhouette.

DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS:
- Highly simplified, geometric heron shape using clean lines and basic shapes
- Single continuous line or minimal stroke design
- Heron shown in elegant standing pose, long neck curved in S-shape
- Colors: Navy blue (#0F1729) for the heron, subtle gold (#D4A853) accent on beak or eye
- Pure white background (#FFFFFF)
- Company name "ScaleUp Ventures" below in elegant serif font (navy color)

STYLE:
- Corporate, sophisticated, timeless
- Must work at 16x16 favicon size
- Saul Bass / Paul Rand aesthetic - geometric reduction
- NOT cartoonish, cute, or illustrative
- Clean vector-style execution

OUTPUT: Professional logo on white background, centered composition
```
