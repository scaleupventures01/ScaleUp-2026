A

THE KEYSTONE

The stone that locks everything in place

Strategic Connection

The keystone is the wedge-shaped stone at the apex of an arch - the LAST stone placed, and the one that locks everything else in place. Without it, the arch collapses. ScaleUp's proof-first approach IS the keystone. Without verified proof, business transformation collapses.

PURE

Keystone Pure

Just the solid gold trapezoid. Maximum simplicity. The one distinctive element.

DIMENSIONAL

Keystone Dimensional

Subtle depth effect with darker gold edge. Implies substance without being 3D.

OUTLINE

Keystone with Outline

Gold fill with thin navy outline. More defined edge for certain applications.

B

THE PIVOT

Where transformation happens

Strategic Connection

A pivot is the single point where transformation happens - where doubt becomes certainty. The horizontal bar represents "stuck," the ascending bar represents "growth," and the gold point represents "the proof moment" - the instant everything changed.

STANDARD

Pivot Standard

Balanced bar thickness with gold pivot point. Clean and professional.

BOLD

Pivot Bold

Thicker bars for more presence and impact. Stronger visual weight.

REFINED

Pivot Refined

Thinner bars for elegance and sophistication. More premium feel.

C

THE BENCHMARK

The point of known truth (Backup Concept)

Strategic Connection

A benchmark is a surveyor's mark - a point of known position from which all other measurements are taken. Before building anything, you need a benchmark. ScaleUp provides that benchmark - verified proof from which all business decisions can be measured.

SOLID

Benchmark Solid

Solid navy triangle with gold benchmark line. Strong, definitive.

OUTLINE

Benchmark Outline

Navy triangle outline with gold line. More open, lighter feel.

Evaluation Criteria (All Must Pass)

SQUINT TEST

At 16px favicon, is mark recognizable?

In pure B&W, does it work?

TRUST TEST

Would a skeptical 45-year-old think "these people are serious"?

STORY TEST

Can you explain meaning in one sentence?

WHITE TEST

Does it look confident on pure white?
