Section 01

Executive Summary

Key Finding: YES - You Can Share Landing Pages

The top tier industries (63+ score) share 80-90% identical characteristics. You can create a master landing page template with modular industry-specific content blocks. The core value proposition, pain points, and service offerings are essentially identical across the top 20 industries.

What's Shared Across All Top Tier Industries:

  • Owner Profile: 50-60 year old, non-technical, family business owner
  • Core Pain: Technology overwhelm, competition from larger players, succession pressure
  • Budget: 5-15% of revenue available for marketing/technology
  • Service Need: Website + SEO + AI/Automation bundle
  • Urgency Driver: "Modernize or be left behind" + "Increase exit value"

What Needs Per-Industry Customization:

  • Statistics: Market size, owner age, satisfaction rates
  • Terminology: "Dispatch" vs "Scheduling" vs "Booking"
  • Association Logos: PHCC, NPMA, ASA, etc.
  • Hero Image: Plumber vs Electrician vs Staffing recruiter
  • Case Studies: Industry-specific testimonials

The "High Scorer DNA"

Top tier industries share a common profile:

  • Older owners (30-55+) who built businesses before the digital era
  • Existential threat (AI disruption, PE consolidation)
  • Available budgets (3-10% of revenue) but don't know how to spend wisely
  • Owner-operators who can make fast decisions without committees
  • Clear congregation points (trade associations, conferences)
