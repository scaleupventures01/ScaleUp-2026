Executive Summary

ScaleUp Ventures is a 1.5-year-old business consulting firm with development capabilities, operating a "studio model" that combines client services with proprietary product development. The company has generated ~$50K in revenue from 4-5 clients but faces margin challenges (23% gross, 3% net) due to early-stage pricing pressure and inconsistent cost management. The founder seeks to reach $100K in 2026 with high margins and minimal personal time involvement, as a stepping stone toward financial independence and a portfolio of scalable assets.
