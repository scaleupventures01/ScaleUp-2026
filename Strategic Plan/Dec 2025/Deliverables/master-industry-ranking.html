Section 07

Scoring Methodology

8-Criteria Framework (80 Points Maximum)

1. Market Size (TAM Potential)
Total addressable market size, number of businesses, and revenue potential. Larger, more fragmented markets score higher.
2. Owner Demographics
Average age of business owners. Older owners (50+) who are less tech-savvy and seeking exit strategies score higher.
3. Budget Availability
Profit margins, discretionary spending capacity, and historical marketing investment levels.
4. Pain Intensity
Severity of operational challenges, disruption fears, and urgency to modernize or face extinction.
5. Competition Level
Saturation of marketing/tech vendors serving this industry. Less competition = higher score.
6. Service Fit
Alignment with ScaleUp's dev, growth marketing, and AI/automation offerings.
7. Acquisition Ease
Availability of congregation points (associations, conferences, publications) for efficient outreach.
8. Sales Cycle
Typical time from first contact to closed deal. Shorter cycles score higher.
