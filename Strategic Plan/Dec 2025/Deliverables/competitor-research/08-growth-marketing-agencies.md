# Growth Marketing Agencies - Competitive Research

**Competitive Analysis for ScaleUp Ventures Growth Services**

*Research Date: December 31, 2025*

---

## Executive Summary

This research identifies 15 named growth marketing agencies competing for SMB clients with $20K+ marketing capacity who value ROI-focused marketing. ScaleUp Ventures differentiates by offering growth services as a cross-sell from development, creating an integrated value proposition that few competitors match.

**Key Competitive Insights:**
- Most agencies specialize in EITHER development OR marketing; few do both well
- Pricing ranges from $750/month (entry-level social media) to $50,000+/month (full-service enterprise)
- Average hourly rates: $100-$199/hr for premium agencies
- ROI focus is a common claim but execution varies widely
- SMB-focused agencies typically have minimum project sizes of $1,000-$10,000

## Strategic Implications for ScaleUp Ventures

### Competitive Advantages to Emphasize

1. **Development-First Approach:** Most marketing agencies bolt on development; ScaleUp builds marketing on top of strong development foundations

2. **Integrated Value Proposition:** Cross-sell from development creates stickier relationships than marketing-only engagements

3. **SMB ROI Focus:** Many competitors target enterprise or VC-backed startups; SMBs with $20K+ capacity are underserved

4. **Pricing Sweet Spot:** Position between budget agencies ($750-2,000/month) and premium agencies ($10,000+/month)

### Competitive Gaps to Exploit

1. **Project-to-Retainer Transition:** Development projects create trust for marketing retainers
2. **Technical Marketing:** Understanding of tracking, attribution, and technical implementation
3. **Startup/SMB Specificity:** Tailored services for companies without marketing teams
4. **Transparent ROI Tracking:** Many agencies claim ROI focus but lack clear measurement

### Threats to Monitor

1. **Full-Service Giants (WebFX, SmartSites):** Scale advantages and brand recognition
2. **Performance Specialists (KlientBoost, Disruptive):** Deep PPC/performance expertise
3. **Startup-Focused Agencies (NoGood, Growthcurve):** VC-backed startup credibility
4. **Budget Players (LYFE Marketing):** Price-sensitive SMB capture
