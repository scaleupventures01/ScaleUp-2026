# Boutique Consulting Firms: Competitive Analysis

**Target Company:** ScaleUp Ventures
**Analysis Date:** December 31, 2025
**Analyst:** Claude Code Research

---

## Executive Summary

This report identifies and analyzes boutique consulting firms that compete directly with ScaleUp Ventures in the SMB consulting space. ScaleUp Ventures differentiates through:
- Integrated services (Development + Growth Marketing + Process Improvement/AI/Automation)
- ROI-focused selling with accountability metrics
- Target market: SMBs with $20K+ investment capacity

**Key Finding:** Most boutique competitors specialize in 1-2 service areas rather than offering truly integrated services. This presents both competitive validation (the market exists) and differentiation opportunity (few offer the full stack with accountability).

## Key Insights and Strategic Implications

### Market Gaps Identified

1. **Few True Full-Stack Competitors:** Most boutique firms specialize in either marketing OR technology OR operations. Very few offer integrated Development + Marketing + Process Improvement.

2. **ROI Accountability is Rare:** While many claim "results-focused," few have explicit accountability metrics or ROI guarantees in their positioning.

3. **AI/Automation is Emerging:** Most traditional boutique consultancies are just beginning to add AI services. Early movers have competitive advantage.

4. **SMB Sweet Spot:** Many firms either target enterprise (too expensive for SMBs) or very small businesses (less sophisticated). The $20K+ investment capacity SMB is underserved.

### ScaleUp Ventures Differentiation Opportunities

1. **Emphasize True Integration:** Position as the only firm offering Development + Growth Marketing + AI/Automation under one roof with unified accountability.

2. **ROI Guarantee Framework:** Develop and market a clear ROI accountability structure that competitors lack.

3. **$20K+ Sweet Spot:** Own the SMB segment with meaningful investment capacity but not enterprise complexity.

4. **AI-First Process Improvement:** Lead with AI/automation expertise as key differentiator vs. traditional consultants.

### Pricing Benchmarks

Based on research:
- **Fractional CMO Services:** $5,000-$15,000/month
- **Fractional CMO Hourly:** $250-$400/hour (experienced)
- **AI Consulting:** $5,000-$15,000 per project; $200-$350/hour
- **Digital Marketing Retainers:** $2,500-$15,000/month for SMBs
- **Development Projects:** $105,000+ for MVP; $200,000+ for full development

### Competitive Response Considerations

1. **Cultivate Advisors** is most similar in holistic approach - differentiate on tech/AI capability
2. **PSM Partners** has similar tech+growth model - differentiate on marketing depth and national reach
3. **Fractional CMO firms** (Chief Outsiders, Astute) have executive expertise - differentiate on implementation + development
4. **AI specialists** (MQLFlow, Zebra) have automation focus - differentiate on strategic business advisory
