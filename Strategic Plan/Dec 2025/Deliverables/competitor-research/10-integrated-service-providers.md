# Competitive Research: Integrated Service Providers
## Development + Marketing + Automation Combined Offerings

**Research Date:** December 31, 2025
**Target Context:** ScaleUp Ventures - integrated provider of Development, Growth Marketing, AND Process Improvement (AI/automation)

---

## Executive Summary

This research identifies agencies and consultancies that offer the same integrated combination of services as ScaleUp Ventures: custom development, growth marketing, and AI/automation process improvement. The "land with dev, expand into growth and process improvement" model is increasingly common, with several established players operating in this space.

**Key Findings:**
- Most integrated providers are HubSpot-centric agencies that expanded from marketing into development
- Pricing ranges dramatically: from $3,200/month (subscription models) to $20,000+/month (enterprise retainers)
- True integration of all three capabilities (dev + marketing + automation) remains relatively rare
- The SMB segment is underserved by quality integrated providers
- "Land and expand" is a validated business model driving 50%+ of revenue for mature agencies

## Key Insights for ScaleUp Ventures

### Market Gaps Identified

1. **SMB Underserved:** Most integrated providers target mid-market to enterprise. True SMB-focused integrated providers are rare.

2. **AI/Automation Innovation Gap:** Many agencies treat automation as an add-on rather than core competency. ScaleUp can differentiate with AI-first positioning.

3. **HubSpot Dependency:** Most competitors are HubSpot-centric. Platform-agnostic providers have room to differentiate.

4. **Custom Dev + Marketing Integration:** Few agencies genuinely excel at both custom software development AND growth marketing. Most are stronger in one.

5. **Land and Expand Validation:** Research confirms this model drives 50%+ of revenue at mature agencies. Cross-service stickiness is a proven retention mechanism.

### Pricing Benchmarks

| Service Level | Monthly Retainer | Project Minimum |
|---------------|------------------|-----------------|
| SMB Entry | $3,200-$5,000 | $5,000-$10,000 |
| SMB Growth | $5,000-$12,000 | $10,000-$25,000 |
| Mid-Market | $12,000-$25,000 | $25,000-$80,000 |
| Enterprise | $25,000-$50,000+ | $50,000-$500,000+ |

### Competitive Advantages ScaleUp Could Leverage

1. **True Integration:** Position as genuinely integrated vs "we also do X"
2. **SMB Accessibility:** Offer enterprise-quality at SMB-accessible pricing
3. **AI-Native:** Built for AI era, not adapting legacy services
4. **Flexible Tech Stack:** Not locked to one platform
5. **Land and Expand:** Formalize the cross-sell motion with clear expansion paths
