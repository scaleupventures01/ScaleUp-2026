Key Strategic Insights
  • Large consulting firms (McKinsey, Deloitte, Accenture) present minimal direct threat - Their $500K+ minimums and Fortune 500 focus leave the SMB market underserved.
  • Boutique consultants are the primary competition - Cultivate Advisors, PSM Partners, and fractional CMO firms compete for similar clients but rarely offer truly integrated services.
  • DIY tools represent the largest substitute threat - Platforms like Webflow, HubSpot, and Zapier enable SMBs to handle work in-house at a fraction of consulting costs.
  • Offshore development competes on price alone - ScaleUp wins on integration, accountability, and reduced management burden vs. $15-50/hr offshore rates.
  • 40% of AI consulting deals under $5M now go to boutiques - Up from 15% in 2023, creating significant opportunity in the growing SMB AI market.
  • Prevyu has no direct competitor - No tool offers real-time pre-trade discipline validation with GO/WAIT/NO-GO signals against personal rules.

ScaleUp Consulting Services
  • Few True Full-Stack Competitors: Most boutique firms specialize in either marketing OR technology OR operations. Very few offer integrated Development + Marketing + Process Improvement.

  • Premium Positioning Justified: At $79-500/mo, positioned above journals ($15-50/mo) because one prevented bad trade can pay for a year of service.
  • Category Creator Opportunity: "Pre-trade discipline technology" is an ownable category position.

Differentiation Strategy

ScaleUp Ventures Key Differentiators
  1. Integrated Multi-Disciplinary Delivery: Single point of accountability across development, marketing, and AI/automation. Competitors require multiple vendors.
  2. Business Outcomes, Not Billable Hours: Project-based pricing tied to results. Most competitors sell time.
  3. Strategic Partnership vs. Task Execution: Consultative approach that includes strategy, prioritization, and business context. Freelancers and offshore shops execute tasks.
  4. True Cost Transparency: All-inclusive project pricing with no hidden management burden. Offshore "savings" often disappear after rework and management costs.
  5. AI-First Process Improvement: Lead with process improvement methodology, apply AI as solution. Most AI consultants lead with technology.
Prevyu Key Differentiators
  1. Pre-Trade Intervention: All competitors analyze AFTER the trade. Prevyu intervenes BEFORE entry.
  2. Personal Rule Validation: Trade Ideas uses THEIR signals. Prevyu validates YOUR rules against YOUR criteria.
  3. Intelligent GO/WAIT/NO-GO: TradeStop provides binary lockouts. Prevyu provides nuanced, intelligent decision support.
  4. Discipline Through Intelligence: Manual checklists can be bypassed by mindlessly clicking. Prevyu can't be gamed.
  5. Category Creator: "Pre-trade discipline technology" is an ownable market position with no direct competitor.
